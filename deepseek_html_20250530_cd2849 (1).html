How It Works

1. Place Your Order

Browse our collections and add items to your cart. Choose your preferred delivery method during checkout.

2. Order Processing

Our team processes your order within 24 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation with your order details.

3. Dispatch

Once processed, your order is carefully packed and dispatched. You'll receive tracking information if applicable.

4. Delivery

Your order arrives at your chosen destination. For Click & Collect, you'll be notified when it's ready for pickup.

Delivery FAQs

What are your delivery hours?

Our standard delivery operates Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm. Express deliveries may be available outside these hours depending on your location.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

You can change your delivery address within 1 hour of placing your order by contacting our customer service. After this window, changes may not be possible if your order has already been processed.

Do you deliver internationally?

Currently, we only deliver within the United States. We're working to expand our delivery options to other countries in the future.

What happens if I'm not home for delivery?

If you're not available, the courier will leave a card with instructions for redelivery or collection. You may also be able to provide delivery instructions during checkout.
